ingredients: 
- 2 c flour 
- ¼ c sugar 
- 3 tsp. Baking Powder 
- ½ tsp.Salt 
- ¼ c shortening 
- 1 egg 
- 1 c milk 
Directions: 
- Sift first four ingredients together. 
- Add remaining ingredients. 
- Cut and mix together. 
- Make batter lumpy. 
- Put in greased muffin pan. 
- Bake at 400-degrees for 20-25 minutes.
