ingredients: 
- 5 small Zucchini 
- ¼ cup Chopped Onion 
- ¼ cup Butter 
- ½ cup Sour Cream 
- 2 tsp. Poppy seed 
- 2 tsp. Paprika 
- 1 tsp. Salt 
Directions: 
- Cook zucchini, onion and butter together until squash is tender. 
- Mix together sour cream, poppy seed, paprika and salt. 
- Stir into the squash mixture. 
- Heat gently through.
