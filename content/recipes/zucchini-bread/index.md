ingredients: 
- 3 Eggs (beaten) 
- 1 cup Oil 
- 2 cups Sugar 
- 2 cups Zucchini (with peeling on, grated) 
- 2 Tbsp. Vanilla 
- 1 tsp. Salt 
- 3 cups Flour 
- 1 tsp Soda 
- 1 tsp Cinnamon 
- Â½ cup Nuts 
Directions: 
- Preheat oven to 325 degrees. 
- Add beaten eggs, oil, sugar, zucchini and vanilla. Cream together. 
- Add dry ingredients to the creamed mixture. Blend well and add nuts. 
- Bake in 2 greased/floured loaf pans for 1 hour or until done.
