ingredients: 
- 3 cups (canned) Sweet Potatoes (drained) 1 cup Sugar 
- ½ tsp.Salt 
- 2 Eggs 
- 4 Tbsp melted Butter or Margarine 
- ½ cup Milk 
- 1 tsp. Vanilla 
- INGREDIENTS FOR TOPPING 
- 1 cup Brown Sugar 
- 2/3 cup Flour 
- 1 cup Chopped Pecans 
- ½ cup melted Butter or Margarine 
Directions: 
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. 
- Combine the first 7 ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Mix well with electric mixer. 
- Pour into 9"x13" baking dish. Set aside. 
- In a small to medium bowl, combine topping ingredients. Mix well with spoon. 
- Using clean hands, sprinkle over the sweet potato mixture the best you can. 
- Bake for 45 minutes. 
- Serve hot or cold.
