ingredients: 
- 3 Eggs (beaten) 
- ½ cup Sugar 
- 1 cup Griffins Waffle Syrup 
- 1/8 tsp. Salt 
- 1 tsp. Vanilla 
- ¼ cup Butter (melted) 
- 1 cup Pecans 
Directions: 
- Beat eggs. Add remaining ingredients,and lightly mix. 
- Pour over pecans in piecrust. 
- Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes.
