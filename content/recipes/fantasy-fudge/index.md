Directions: 
- Mix together sugar & evaporated milk and boil for 4 minutes. 
- Add chocolate bars, marshmallow cream and chocolate chips and mix together thoroughly. 
- Add nuts to taste. 
- Pour on buttered platter. 
- Cool and cut into squares. Serve.
