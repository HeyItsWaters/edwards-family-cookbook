ingredients: 
- 1 ½ cup Lean Hamburger 
- 1 cup Finely Chopped Mushrooms 
- 1 large Onion ( finely chopped) 
- 3 Tbsp. Butter 
- 1 ½ cup Sour Cream 
- 1 ½ tsp. Salt 
- ¼ tsp. Pepper 
- 2 cups Flour 
- 1 Tbsp. Sugar 
- ½ cup Shortening 
- 1 Egg (separated) 
Directions: 
- Saute hamburger, mushrooms and onion in butter until meat is browned. 
- Remove from heat. Drain. 
- Stir in ½ cup sour cream, ½ tsp salt and pepper. 
- Mix flour, remaining salt and sugar in a bowl. Cut in shortening. 
- Mix eggyolk and remaining sour cream. 
- Add to flour mixture. Mix well. 
- Roll out on a floured surface. 
- Cut into circles with sour cream carton. 
- Place 1 heaping teaspoon filling in the middle of each circle of pastry. Fold over. Seal edges. 
- Mix egg white with 1 Tbsp. water. Brush over pies. 
- Place on a baking sheet. Bake in a preheated 400-degree oven for 15-20 minutes.
