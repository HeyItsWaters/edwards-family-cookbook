ingredients: 
- 2 packages ( 6 oz. each) Butterscotch Pieces 
- 1 cup Spanish Peanuts (NOT-RAW) 
- 1-2 cups Chow Mien Noodles 
Directions: 
- Melt butterscotch. 
- Add noodles and peanuts until well coated. 
- Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper. Chill until firm.
