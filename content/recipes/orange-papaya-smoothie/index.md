ingredients:
- 1 ripe Papaya (peeled, seeded and chopped)
- 2 cups Orange Juice
- 1 cup Plain Yogurt
- 1 cup Cracked Ice
- 1 Banana (mashed)
- 1 tsp. Lime or Lemon juice

directions:
- Puree or process all ingredients until smooth.
- Serve in tall glasses.
