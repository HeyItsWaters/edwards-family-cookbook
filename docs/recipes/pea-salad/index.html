Pea Salad

Submitted by: Grandma Ruth

Ingredients

  • 2 cans Sweet Peas{drained)
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Sweet Pickles
  • Onion
  • Boiled Egg
  • Miracle Whip

Directions

  • Empty peas in large mixing bowl.
  • Cut cheese into small cubes.
  • Chop pickles, onion.s and egg. Add all 4 ingredients to peas.
  • Fold in Miracle Whip to taste.
