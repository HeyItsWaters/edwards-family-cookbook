Easy Church Punch

Submitted by: Jeannie (Mom)

Ingredients

  • 1 package Unsweetened Lemonade Kool-Aid
  • 2 packages Unsweetened Raspberry Kool-Aid
  • Water to taste
  • 1 Large Can Pineapple Juice
  • 3 Liter bottle 7-up or Sprite

Directions

  • Mix well in very large container.
  • Chill.
  • Serve.
